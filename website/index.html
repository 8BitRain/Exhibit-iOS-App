What we do at Exhibit

Exhibit is a cross-platform mobile application that explores the new and exciting experience of view homes, apartments and other properties through virtual reality . With a few swipes, users can navigate an intuitive search feature and instantly find themselves standing in the property they want to see. Users can look around as if they're actually standing at the property; this is made possible through the sophisticated use of gyroscopic sensors and projective geometry. For sellers, adding listings is also a piece of cake through Parse. From home owners to budding businesses, Exhibit aims to bring the best places, to the best people, in the best way.
